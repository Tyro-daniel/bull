Our Story

A Gaborone institution unlike any other

Nestled in the heart of Gaborone, Bull & Bush has long been the city's favourite gathering place — where great food, cold drinks, and genuine Botswana warmth come together under one roof.

From sizzling grills to hearty pub classics, every dish is crafted with care, served with pride, and best enjoyed in good company.
